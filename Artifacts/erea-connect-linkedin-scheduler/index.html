What does it look like when a Belgian industrial distributor decides to get serious about LinkedIn? We're about to find out. Over the last few months I've been building the GTM foundation for Herman Nollet and the team at EREA — a technical wholesale distributor of electrical installation materials, active in Belgium, the Netherlands, France, and Germany. Strong reputation. Loyal customers. Nearly invisible to new prospects online. Here's what we built — EREA Connect: → Prospect database: 5,800+ mapped contacts across 4 markets and 4 countries → 4 ICP segments defined: technical directors, consultants, procurement leads, and specialists → Personalised LinkedIn outreach sequences in NL, FR, EN, and DE — one per segment → Full playbook: strategy, messaging guide, and execution framework — transferable to their team The goal isn't mass outreach. It's systematic, personal, relevant conversations — at scale. What makes this interesting in technical B2B: your buyers are on LinkedIn. They just haven't heard from you yet. EREA Connect is about to change that. Thanks @Herman Nollet for the trust, and @Tunity for coordinating the execution.
