Section 1 · Hormozi rule — find what they WANT, not what they need

The ONE biggest pain B2B SMB founders, CEOs and CMOs face in 2026.

Convergent signal across McKinsey, Gartner, HubSpot State of Marketing 2026, Pavilion CRO Summit, Deloitte CMO Survey, Dunford, Walker, FounderOS and the AI-native GTM agency reports.

The pain, in Phil's voice "Pipeline is invisible and unpredictable — and the old playbook is making it worse."

The three sub-pains that ladder up to it

48%
of B2B buyers use GenAI (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) to discover vendors — and shortlist you before you know they exist.
~50%
of stalled deals die in "no decision" — not lost to a competitor. The buyer just freezes.
+60%
CAC inflation over 5 years. Performance marketing is commoditised. Volume is dead.
81%
of B2B buyers decide on vendors BEFORE engaging sales. The funnel happens off your site.
11
average stakeholders in a B2B buying committee. 77% research independently first.
5x
AI-referred visitors convert at 14.2% vs 2.8% for Google organic. GEO is the new SEO.
#1
Brand awareness just beat lead generation as CMO priority — first time in HubSpot history.
94%
of CMOs report no meaningful AI integration. The integration gap is the moat.
What they say they need (and is now wrong)

"We need more leads. We need better content. We need a new agency."

What they actually want: A pipeline they can defend on Friday — predictable, visible, board-ready — that doesn't depend on them, the CEO, the agency, or one channel.
